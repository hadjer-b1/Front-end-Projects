Lasagna Recipe

A rich and comforting Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy béchamel!

Let's make it!

There's something timeless about a bubbling tray of lasagna fresh out of the oven. Layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, tender pasta sheets, and melty cheese, this Italian classic is more than just comfort food—it’s a whole experience. Whether you're making it for a cozy family dinner, meal prep for the week, or a special gathering, lasagna brings people together with every hearty bite. In this recipe, we’re walking you through how to make it step-by-step, with tips to make it as delicious as any you've had in a trattoria in Italy.

Preparation

Ingredients For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until thickened.

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (warm)
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until light golden.
  3. Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps
  4. Cook until thick, then season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg

For the Lasagna Layers:

  • 9-12 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or no-boil)
  • 150g mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 50g Parmesan cheese (grated)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
  3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
  4. Spread a layer of béchamel sauce and sprinkle mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and cheese on top.
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese.

Final Steps "Bake":

  1. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  3. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
